    What is statistical coupling analysis?


    SCA offers the potential to identify secondary residues by identifying correlations between changes in protein sequences.


    For instance, in a sequence alignment, residues that are not highly conserved may initially seem insignificant. However, by investigating the connectivity of these residues, SCA can reveal interesting relationships between them.
