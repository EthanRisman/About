    • Sequence Read Archive
    The SRA hosts sequencing data from thousands of research studies, covering a range of organisms, tissue types, experimental conditions, and sequencing technologies. Researchers can access this data for free, allowing them to explore existing datasets without the need to generate new data. Amateurs such as myself can download any dataset from the SRA to practice alignment, variant calling, or differential expression analysis as well as dozens of bioinformatics tools.
