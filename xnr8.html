    • Homology based prediction using Exonerate
    Lets say you want to want to find commonly conserved genes in a draft or novel genome. A reliable, albeit dated approach is to use exonerate. This tool can compare nucleotide or amino acid sequences against a genome, and return the best hits within.

    Toy diagram of a exonerate Protein2Genome pipeline. A Starting with a genome (black) and proteins sequences from online DBs. B Exonerate finding the best alignments (hits) along the genome, note that some protein sequences will return multiple hits. Exonerate also works with soft-masking repretitive regions (white gaps). C The best hits are validated through another alignment, and can be investigated at a higher order.
